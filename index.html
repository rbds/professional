Welcome to my GitHub Page.

I am a graduate student in Mechanical Engineering at George Washington University. My focus is in robotics, controls, and mechatronics. On this page you'll find a sample of the work I have done, including classwork, research, and personal projects.

Research

In the spirit of Thing Explainer (https://xkcd.com/1133/ uses the one thousand most common words in English to explain complex mechanisms, machines, and ideas): I help tell machines how to get from place to place without running into anything and without running out of power.

My research is in energy-focused navigation algorithms for mobile robots. Much of the work takes place in MATLAB simulations, for which you can find the code here: https://github.com/rbds/Research. We also have a robotic test platform which uses an Arduino for control and a Raspberry Pi for computing to test out our algorithms in real environments.
